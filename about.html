About the house

  • The living room has a gas fireplace, wireless Internet and high-definition smart TV with Netflix.

  • The loft bathroom includes a double whirlpool overlooking the woods and a large stone-lined shower with rainfall head, steam, spray bar and bench.

  • The upstairs bedroom has a queen bed and is flooded with morning sun.

  • The dining area overlooks the back yard.

  • The kitchen has a dishwasher, microwave and gas stove plus lots of cookware and serving dishes.

  • This sunny, lower-level room has a full bed and stays cool all summer. It has a private entrance through the garage.

  • This lower-level room has a twin bed with a pull-out trundle bed and adjoins the second bathroom.

  • The house sits on a quarter acre and includes a stream and small woods.

Location

The house is right off Woodland Avenue, three miles up the hill from Lake Superior and the 21st Avenue East exit from I-35. It's a 10-minute drive from Canal Park.

It's between Hartley Park and Forest Hill Cemetery, so there's a lot of wildlife traffic, including the occasional bear. In fall, deer come to eat apples from two trees.

From the house, it's a five-mile walk on the Superior Hiking Trail  to the Rose Garden, through Hartley Park, Bagley Park and Chester Park.

The No. 13 bus passes the end of the street and goes to the University of Minnesota-Duluth and downtown.
