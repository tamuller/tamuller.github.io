About our house

The two-bedroom, two-bath house sits on a quarter acre with woods and a small stream. It's a block from the Superior Hiking Trail and Hartley Park.

The house feels like a cabin, with a loft that includes a master suite with a large stone-lined steam shower and a double whirlpool overlooking the woods. It has large, double-paned casement windows throughout.

In the living room, there's a gas fireplace and high-definition smart TV.

The kitchen includes a gas range, microwave and dishwasher and is completely outfitted with dishes, cookware and serving ware.

The lower level includes a sunny bedroom with entrance through the garage, a second bathroom with shower, a bonus office/bedroom and a washer and dryer.

There are patios with perennial gardens in front and also in the fenced side yard.

The house is just off Woodland Avenue and is easily reached by car or the No. 13 bus to UMD and downtown.
